### Commonly Referenced Competencies

### KA-Specific Scopes (Key Phrases)
- **Software Architecture KA**: Structural organization, stakeholder concerns, representation methods, evaluation techniques[1].
- **Software Design KA**: Transforming requirements into blueprints, design concepts, processes, quality attributes[1].
- **Software Engineering Management KA**: Planning, coordination, measurement, monitoring, control, reporting; balancing technical/project constraints[1].
- **Software Engineering Process KA**: Definition, implementation, assessment, measurement, improvement; life cycle models, frameworks[1].
- **Software Engineering Models KA**: Modeling notations, methodological frameworks for systematic/repeatable activities[1].
